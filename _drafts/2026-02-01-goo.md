In a time of information overload and non-regulation, it has become difficult to distill that which is true from what has been distorted; and it seems the former is at a loss. Several epiphanies lead me to deem this a positive; well aware of the pitfalls of misinformation, the lack of true regulation in the world wild web has however the capacity to take power from those who may be ill intentioned and give it back to the people. The main epiphany is simple, for it exposes the made up nature of civilisation, if those with nothing more than a loud platform can make up information to induce mass responses of a desired nature, then what is stopping myself from doing the same? Moreover, as a person susceptible to information, what is stopping me from bending reality to induce desired changes in my own self i.e. if the world is what you make it, then how do you become the change you want to see? Luckily, a friend introduced me to the most reasonable method i have yet encountered towards inducing such change in selves; it is that of cognitive hypnotherapy as described by Trevor Sylvester.
